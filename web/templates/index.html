Frequently Asked Questions

  • How To Buy Data?

    1. Log in to your account
    2. If not; register a new account
    3. After log in click fund my account if low balance
    4. Fill the Data form and proceed with the next actions.

  • What Are The Codes For Checking Data Balance ?

    1. MTN (SME)-> *461*4#
    2. MTN (GIFTING)-> *131*4# or *460*260#
    3. 9mobile[SME]-> *229*9#
    4. 9mobile[Gifting]-> *228#
    5. Airtel-> *140#
    6. Glo-> *127*0#.

  • How Do I Fund My Wallet?

      We have provided you some easy way to get money into your account:

    1. Bank payment for manual funding.
    2. Online Payment with your ATM card details via Pay stack Payment Gateway.

  • How To Use Our Services?

    1. STEP 1: Fund your wallet.
    2. STEP 2: Fill the data order form.
    3. STEP 3: Wait for 1-15 minutes, the recipient will receive notification(s) of data recharge(Except for 9mobile(SME), which should be confirmed with its balance code)

  • Airtime Transfer Codes?

    1. (MTN) => *600*phone_number*amount*pin#
    2. (9mobile) => *223*PIN*Amount*Phone Number#
    3. (Airtel) *432# > Select 1 (Airtel to Airtel) > Enter Airtel Number > Enter Amount > Enter Pin > Press Send/Ok
    4. (Glo) *131*phone_number*amount*pin#.
